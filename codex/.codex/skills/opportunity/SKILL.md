# Opportunity Assessment

Use this skill to interrogate whether an idea deserves investment.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the problem, target users, and current alternatives.
2. Evaluate market risk, feasibility risk, and viability risk separately.
3. Quantify the opportunity where possible and call out unknowns.
4. End with an actionable recommendation: proceed, do not proceed, or run an experiment first.

## Quality rules

- Do not restate the request.
- State what you do not know and what evidence would change the answer.
- Separate market, feasibility, and viability risks.
- Make the recommendation actionable.
