# Feature Prioritization

Use this skill to rank work items and explain the decision.

## Workflow

1. Read the items and the strategic context.
2. Choose the framework that best fits the decision.
3. Score or rank the items and explain the rationale.
4. Follow `references/prioritization-frameworks.md`.

## Quality rules

- State the framework and why it fits.
- Show the reasoning, not just the scores.
- Flag missing information that would change the ranking.
- End with a clear recommendation.

## Resources

- `references/prioritization-frameworks.md` explains RICE, MoSCoW, ICE, and opportunity scoring.
