# Churn Analysis and Reduction

Use this skill to turn retention data into a concrete reduction plan.

## Workflow

1. Break churn into voluntary vs involuntary and logo vs revenue churn.
2. Classify cancellation reasons with the churn taxonomy.
3. Identify observable at-risk signals that predict cancellation.
4. Propose interventions by churn type, including cancellation flow, win-back, and dunning.

## Quality rules

- Separate logo churn from revenue churn.
- Base at-risk signals on product data, not gut feel.
- Keep the cancel flow honest and fast.
- Map each intervention to a specific churn cause.
