# Customer Onboarding Design

Use this skill to design onboarding that moves users to activation quickly.

## Workflow

1. Read the product context and identify where onboarding breaks down.
2. Define the activation milestone and onboarding funnel.
3. Design the in-product flow and email sequence.
4. Follow `references/onboarding-guide.md`.

## Quality rules

- The activation milestone must be measurable.
- Every step should support the critical path to activation.
- Emails should be behavior-triggered when possible.
- Empty states should guide the next action.

## Resources

- `references/onboarding-guide.md` covers activation milestones, funnel stages, email sequencing, and success metrics.
