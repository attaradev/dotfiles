# Competitive Analysis

Use this skill to compare the market honestly and identify a defensible position.

## Workflow

1. Build the player map: direct, indirect, and non-consumption alternatives.
2. Compare positioning, feature coverage, strengths, and weaknesses.
3. Identify market gaps and underserved demand.
4. Derive a specific differentiation angle the team can defend.
5. Save the output to `docs/competitive-<slug>.md` when useful.

## Quality rules

- Name specific competitors.
- Distinguish direct competitors from substitutes.
- Keep the feature matrix factual.
- Make the differentiation angle specific and defensible.

- `references/competitive-framework.md` contains the analysis structure, player classification, positioning dimensions, and differentiation strategy.
