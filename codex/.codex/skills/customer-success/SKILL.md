# Customer Success Playbook

Use this skill to design a CS model that supports retention and expansion.

## Workflow

1. Segment the CS model by ARR band and touch level.
2. Define health score signals from actual product usage and account data.
3. Specify intervention plays for red, yellow, and green accounts.
4. Add expansion triggers, QBR structure, and CS-to-sales handoff rules.

## Quality rules

- Tie segmentation to ARR bands.
- Base health scores on real product data.
- Trigger expansion from usage signals, not calendar check-ins.
- Make the QBR drive a decision or commitment.
