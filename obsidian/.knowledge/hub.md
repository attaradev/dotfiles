## Weekly Workflow

1. Capture active execution in `tasks.md`.
2. Update milestone status in `projects/projects.md`.
3. Update course pipeline and progress in `learning/courses.md`.
4. Log study sessions in `learning/studies.md`.
5. Track reading pipeline updates in `reading/books.md` and `reading/articles.md`.
6. Record mistakes and new rules in `learning/lessons.md` with source links (reading/course/project) where relevant.
7. Capture outcomes and impact in `career/achievement-log.md`.
